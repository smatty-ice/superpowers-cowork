# Planning

Plan phase. Turn the brief into an ordered, actionable plan.

1. Give each step: what to do, which tools, "done when" criterion, effort estimate.
2. Group steps between checkpoints where the human reviews and can redirect.
3. Mark steps that can run in parallel.
4. Specify the exact tool for every step that uses an external tool.
5. Present with a summary: "[N] steps, [M] checkpoints, estimated [effort]. Ready to start?"

## The Hard Stop

After presenting the plan:

1. Show the plan.
2. Ask: "Ready to start?"
3. **Stop. Do nothing else. Do not begin step 1 until the human explicitly approves.**

The plan exists to be approved, not to be a formality before execution begins.

For the plan template, granularity guide, and effort estimation, read `references/planning-guide.md`.
